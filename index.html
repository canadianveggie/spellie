Spellie is a daily world puzzle geared at young spellers.

Features

  • Age appropriate puzzles with 3 difficulty levels
  • Easy mode has short words within the Grade 2 vocabulary
  • Hard mode is challenging, but uses words within the Grade 5 vocabulary
  • Kid-friendly visuals
  • Emoji collectables

How to play

You have six (6) tries to guess the daily word, using the feedback to narrow down the options.

  • The letter W is in the word and in the right spot.
  • The letter R is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • The letters O and D are not in the word in any spot.
